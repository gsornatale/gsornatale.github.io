.about .me

This site is a random collection of my interests. A digital journal of sorts... a place for me to share anything. If you've found it, welcome! I hope you can find inspiration in a post or two.

Los Angles is my home. I love to listen to music. I love to experience music. I love to find music. I love to make music. I love to support music in any and every way I can.

I love food. I can appreciate the hell out of a tasing menu and can whip up a pretty dope meal. Rice is my favorite food.

I'll never write a negative review. What kind of loser complains on the internet?

I take the road less traveled and I’m always down for a good adventure. Spontaneity has gotten me to the places I’ve been and will likely take me to the place I’ll go.

I ask ‘why’ a lot… because, well, ‘because’ just isn’t enough. I like to challenge myself. I am a man of many talents and even more interests.

More recently I taught myself how to code.. and truthfully I’ll probably never code again and I'll move on to the next random skill I want to saturate my brain cells with. I could’ve used square space… but what fun is that? Shout outs to codecademy and Wes Bos! Good looks.

If I care about it - I’ll get it done. I’m motivated by results.
